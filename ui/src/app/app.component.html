AI Healthcare Bot

An AI healthcare bot is a powerful tool that can assist patients with booking and managing appointments, detecting diseases based on symptoms, map appointments to the appropriate doctor and department, and providing basic first aid information. With its advanced capabilities, the bot can improve patient experience, reduce healthcare costs, and increase efficiency in the healthcare industry.

Manage appointments

An AI chatbot can assist you with managing your appointments by providing a hassle-free experience for booking, rescheduling or cancelling appointments.

First Aid

AI chatbot can provide basic first aid information. Its guidance can help users manage minor injuries or illnesses

Find Suitable Department

AI healthcare chatbot can find the suitable medical department based on the symptoms.**However, patients should always seek the advice of a healthcare professional for an accurate diagnosis**

Admin Area

An administrator who has the ability to train the chatbot model, manage doctors and departments, and view bookings. This provides the administrator with the flexibility to customize and manage the chatbot's functionality to meet the needs of the healthcare organization.
